Attendance Tracking System




Automated Facial Recognition System for classroom attendance, leveraging cutting-edge technology to capture real-time classroom images at 10-minute intervals. This innovative system harnesses advanced facial detection algorithms to precisely mark students' attendance by detecting and distinguishing their faces with remarkable accuracy. Through the integration of FaceNet and MTCNN Models, we ensured robust facial recognition capabilities, enabling seamless identification in dynamic classroom environments.

To enhance the system's efficacy, a comprehensive dataset comprising images of 114 MCA students in diverse poses was meticulously curated. This dataset served as the cornerstone for training and refining the facial recognition model, resulting in an impressive 90% accuracy rate during initial testing. Furthermore, ongoing refinement efforts and future plans include the implementation of dynamic thresholding techniques and dataset augmentation strategies, aimed at continuously improving performance and adaptability.

In parallel, a dynamic web application was developed using Flask, a powerful framework for Python, to complement the facial recognition system. This user-friendly web interface provides educators with intuitive tools for attendance tracking and management, facilitating seamless access to attendance records and enhancing overall administrative efficiency.
